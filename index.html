We have all seen the Despicable Me Series—and loved them, mainly because of the tiny, yellow, bespectacled creatures called Minions.

Speaking like a Minion isn’t only a matter of making incomprehensible sounds with words from different languages thrown in frequently. If observed carefully, Minionese has a lot of resemblance with baby-talk. Anyone who has heard babies cooing in the first years of development will notice that they speak the alphabets “B” and “P” a lot—as do Minions when they say “ba-na-na”, talking in cute little voices with uplifting tones. Just like babies do.
